Vegetarian Tacos

Home

Ingredients

  • 6 small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans (rinsed)
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • Lettuce or cabbage (shredded)
  • Lime, salt, and pepper

Instructions

  1. Warm tortillas in a pan.
  2. Heat beans and corn with a bit of salt and pepper.
  3. Fill tortillas with bean mix, tomato, lettuce, and avocado.
  4. Squeeze lime on top and serve.
