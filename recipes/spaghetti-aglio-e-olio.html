Spaghetti Aglio e Olio (Garlic and Oil Pasta)

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes, then toss in drained spaghetti.
  4. Mix well, season, and top with parsley.
