Chicken Curry

Home

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and tomato paste, stir for 1 min.
  3. Add chicken, cook until browned.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, simmer for 20-25 mins.
  5. Serve with rice.
