Chocolate Chip Cookie

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then mix in egg and vanilla.
  3. Add flour and baking soda, then fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 mins.
